✉️ A Letter to Our Future Investors

Dear Future Investor,

Let’s be honest — most people want to build wealth, but very few ever get to own the engine that moves it.

At Rebtor, we’re changing that story.

Welcome to the Rebtor Fleet Circle™, a well-structured opportunity designed for people who are ready to move beyond wishful thinking and into strategic action. This program is for those who want to put their money into real assets, real operations, and real returns — not another get-rich-quick scheme. We don’t do hype. We do haulage.

🚛 The Big Idea

Our plan is beautifully simple. We bring like-minded investors together into structured clusters, and each cluster co-owns a dump truck, operated by our seasoned trucking experts. The truck is registered in the cluster’s name, and every member of that circle enjoys daily returns — transparently and reliably.

“The rich don’t work for money. They make money work for them.” — Robert Kiyosaki

🛣️ Why This Is Different

Truck ownership traditionally requires huge capital, trusted mechanics, operations management, financial tracking — the list goes on. That’s where we come in. We do the heavy lifting: procurement, registration, driver recruitment, maintenance, reporting, and daily disbursements. You do the smart thing: invest and watch your capital go to work.

This is not an overnight success scheme — it’s a practical way to build long-term wealth in a thriving industry with consistent demand.

“If you don't find a way to make money while you sleep, you will work until you die.” — Warren Buffett

👷🏽‍♂️ What We Ask From You

Come in ready to grow. Come in with patience. Come in with the mindset of a co-owner, not a gambler. This isn’t a bet — it’s a business. You’ll be in good company with others who want returns that are fair, consistent, and rooted in work that moves Nigeria forward.

It’s time to make your money move — not in some abstract app, but on real roads with real results.

Warm regards,

Olutayo Ogunlade
