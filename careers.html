Careers at Rebtor Fleet Circle™

Why Work With Us?

  • Competitive salaries and growth opportunities
  • Training and career development programs
  • Safe, well-maintained trucks and equipment
  • Supportive and professional work environment

Available Positions

Location: Nationwide
Employment Type: Full-time / Contract

Responsibilities:

  • Operate company or partner trucks for local or interstate deliveries.
  • Ensure timely and safe transportation of goods to assigned destinations.
  • Carry out daily vehicle checks and report any mechanical issues immediately.
  • Maintain accurate trip logs and delivery records.
  • Comply with road safety rules and company driving policies.

Requirements:

  • Minimum SSCE Certificate.
  • Valid Class G or E driver’s licence.
  • Proven experience driving heavy-duty trucks (2–5 years preferred).
  • Good knowledge of Nigerian road networks and traffic regulations.
  • Ability to work under minimal supervision.
  • Integrity, discipline, and excellent customer service attitude.

Location: Lagos
Employment Type: Full-time

Responsibilities:

  • Manage daily operations of the company’s fleet.
  • Assign drivers and trucks for scheduled trips.
  • Monitor vehicle performance, fuel usage, and route efficiency.
  • Ensure trucks undergo timely servicing and repairs.
  • Maintain accurate fleet records and generate performance reports.
  • Implement safety policies to reduce accidents and downtime.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree/HND in Transport Management, Logistics, or related field.
  • 3–5 years proven experience in fleet management.
  • Strong leadership, organisational, and decision-making skills.
  • Proficiency in fleet tracking software and Microsoft Office.
  • Good knowledge of Nigerian transport regulations.

Employment Type: Full-time
