🔧 Truck Procurement Made Simple

At Rebtor, we understand how complex and risky truck procurement can be. That’s why we’ve built a trusted end-to-end service designed to help our clients—whether first-time buyers or fleet investors—purchase trucks confidently and cost-effectively.

We partner with reputable companies that deal in various types of trucks and parts, giving our clients access to affordable prices without compromising quality. Whether you’re buying a brand-new vehicle or a reliable foreign-used truck, we help you secure the right asset for your needs.

Our work doesn’t stop at sourcing. We have experienced mechanics on our team who assist in inspecting, assessing, and verifying trucks before any payments are made—so you’re never left in the dark or defrauded.

We also partner with trusted insurance companies to ensure your newly acquired truck is protected from day one, and we handle all documentation, licensing, and registration processes on your behalf.

From finding the truck, to inspecting it, to securing the plate number—we take care of it all. With Rebtor, truck procurement is not just easy, it’s worry-free.

🔍 How Our Truck Procurement Works

📋

Step 1: Tell Us What You Need

Let us know your truck specifications—tipper, flatbed, low bed, or more. We’ll guide you through the best options based on your budget and purpose.

🔧

Step 2: Expert Inspection

Our experienced mechanics and procurement team inspect trucks from our trusted partners to ensure quality, durability, and value before you make payment.

📑

Step 3: Seamless Documentation

We handle all paperwork—licensing, registration, insurance, and ownership documentation. You get a road-ready truck, fully compliant with Nigerian laws.

🤝

Step 4: Handover & Delivery

Once everything is in place, we finalize your ownership and deliver the truck. You receive a detailed performance brief and post-delivery guidance.

🚛

Step 5: Optional Management

If you wish, we can also place your truck under our trusted Rebtor management—handling jobs, drivers, fueling, and daily earnings tracking.
