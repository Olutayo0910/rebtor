Howo Heavy Duty Truck Prices in Nigeria 2025

In 2025, Howo heavy duty trucks remain a popular choice for Nigerian haulage and construction businesses due to their durability, availability of spare parts, and strong diesel engines. Whether you’re looking for a brand-new truck or a foreign-used option, understanding current market prices will help you make the right investment decision. This guide provides the latest Howo truck prices in Nigeria for 2025, including tractor heads, dump trucks, and CNLG models.

Why Choose Howo Heavy Duty Trucks?

Howo trucks, manufactured by the China National Heavy Duty Truck Group (CNHTC), are built to handle Nigeria’s demanding terrain and heavy loads. They are commonly used for:

  • Long-distance cargo haulage
  • Construction material transport
  • Container and port operations
  • Mining and agricultural logistics

Factors Affecting Howo Truck Prices

  • Exchange Rates: The naira-to-dollar exchange rate heavily influences import prices.
  • Specifications: Horsepower, container capacity, and axle configuration affect costs.
  • Condition: New, foreign-used, and refurbished units differ in price.
  • Model Year: Recent models tend to be more expensive.

Final Thoughts

Buying a Howo heavy duty truck in Nigeria is a major investment. Always source from trusted dealers to ensure you get genuine value for your money. While brand-new models offer the latest features and longest service life, well-maintained foreign-used trucks can be a cost-effective alternative.
